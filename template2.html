The Caspian Europe Forum convenes leaders from business, government, and civil society to accelerate sustainable economic cooperation between the Caspian region and Europe.

Established 2018 • Non-Profit Foundation • Geneva Headquarters

Driving sustainable prosperity through collaborative leadership

We are an independent international organization fostering dialogue and partnerships to unlock the economic potential of the Eurasian region. Through strategic initiatives, research, and high-level convenings, we work to create shared value and sustainable growth.
